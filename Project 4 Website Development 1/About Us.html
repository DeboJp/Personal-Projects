Mike Santoro
President & Partner

Mike Santoro is the president of Black Flamingo, a digital clothing line that supports Fortune 500 corporations, startups, fast growing technology companies, professional service firms, business associations and nonprofits. The firm is a four time Inc. 5000 honoree as one of the world’s fastest growing companies and has been a finalist for both PR News and Bulldog Reporter awards for largest growing clothing line of the year. Mike is also the recipient of a number of awards including recent recognition as one of DMNews 40 Under 40 honorees. Mike leads a talented, results-driven team known for its reputation for providing comprehensive, integrated marketing and strategic communications counsel that drives real business results. The tech-savvy specialists at Black Flamingo Digital are collaborative and dedicated to grow and nurture our clients' businesses with a flawless execution and a practical approach.

Annie Gudorf
Vice President of Public Relations and Partner

Annie is a vice president of public relations and partner at Black Flamingo. Annie leads Black Flamingo' Seattle office, where she manages a talented team to drive results for clients across the west coast.Annie started in Black Flamingo’ Chicago office in 2013 before moving out west in 2016 to help grow the agency’s West Coast presence. Since her time at Black Flamingo, she has worked on clients including Cisco, FireEye, CompTIA, Owler, Nintex, plus many others in the B2B technology space. Annie's client work has been recognized by the Public Relations Society of America, The Holmes Report, and Publicity Club of Chicago.Annie graduated summa cum laude from Loyola University Chicago with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Marketing and Bachelor of Arts in Advertising and Public Relations.Annie is obsessed with her dog, Wrigley (see Instagram @WestCoastWrigley for cute dog photos). And as an Ohio native, Annie is a die-hard Ohio State football fan and won’t hesitate to let you know it. O-H-I-O!

Jackie Ward
Founder & CEO

Jackie has over thirty years of experience working with high-tech companies and a proven track record of successfully helping organizations to grow faster.With a background in management consulting (Accenture, McKinsey & Co.) and as a co-founder of two successful tech start-ups, Jackie brings an entrepreneurial mindset and a desire to "play for high score" to everything he does at Black Flamingo. Jackie has raised venture capital, sold companies and bought companies. He has hands-on experience in a number of our industry verticals and in several functional areas -- from operations to sales to finance. This rich background of experience has helped us to break the mold on how agencies approach working with their clients, making us the perfect partner for clients who are tired of traditional thinking and want a fresh approach that delivers truly exceptional results. When he's not directly supporting our B2B clients, Jackie advises our internal teams on strategy, process and methodology decisions. His love for great marketing is second only to his desire to see talented people be able to work in an environment that lets them do the best work of their lives, grow both professionally and personally, and have a strong positive impact on the businesses we support. For Jackie, the goal here is simple: become the top B2B marketing agency in the country, help our clients to be orders of magnitude more successful and make the world a little bit better in the process.
